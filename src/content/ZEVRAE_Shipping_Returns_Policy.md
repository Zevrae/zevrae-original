## RETURN POLICY

### Return Window by Product Category

ZEVRAE's return and exchange windows vary depending on the product category to maintain hygiene, product quality, and customer satisfaction.

#### Clothing (T-Shirts, Jackets, Sweatshirts, Crop Tops & Lowers)

- **Return Window:** Within **2 days** from the date of delivery
- **Exchange Window:** Within **2 days** from the date of delivery
- **Reason:** Clothing items require hygiene standards for returns

#### Accessories (Keychains, Fashion Accessories, etc.)

- **Return Window:** Within **1 day** from the date of delivery
- **Exchange Window:** Within **1 day** from the date of delivery
- **Reason:** Accessories have strict hygiene and quality requirements

#### Jewellery (Rings, Chains, Necklaces, Bracelets, Earrings)

- **Return and exchange eligibility will be determined according to the product category and hygiene requirements**
- **Details:** Refer to ZEVRAE's Jewellery Return & Exchange Policy (separate document)
- **Reason:** Jewellery has specific hygiene, authenticity, and hallmark considerations

### Eligibility Criteria for Returns

To qualify for a return or exchange, the product **must meet ALL of the following criteria:**

#### 1. Condition Requirements

- ✓ **Unused & Unworn:** Product must not have been worn, used, or tried on
- ✓ **Unwashed:** Product must not be washed, dry-cleaned, or altered
- ✓ **Original Tags & Labels:** All original tags, labels, and stickers must be intact and uncut
- ✓ **Packaging:** Original packaging, boxes, tissue paper, and inserts must be intact
- ✓ **Accessories:** All original accessories, complimentary items, and inclusions must be present
- ✓ **Cleanliness:** Product must be free from stains, marks, odors, discoloration, or residue
- ✓ **Physical Integrity:** No tears, rips, scratches, pilling, seam damage, or signs of damage
- ✓ **Color & Quality:** No color fading, discoloration, or manufacturing defects beyond normal manufacturing tolerance

#### 2. Timeline Requirements

- ✓ Return request must be initiated within the specified window (2 days for clothing, 1 day for accessories)
- ✓ Physical product must be received at ZEVRAE within the return window or shortly after

#### 3. Authenticity Verification

- ✓ Product must match the original item shipped by ZEVRAE (SKU verification)
- ✓ Product must be an authentic ZEVRAE item (not counterfeit or third-party item)

### Return Process (Step-by-Step)

**Step 1: Initiate Return Request**

Contact ZEVRAE Customer Support within the return window:

- **Email:** zevareofficial@gmail.com
- **Subject Line:** "RETURN REQUEST - [Order Number]"

Provide the following information:
- Order Number
- Product name and SKU
- Reason for return (size, fit, color, preference, etc.)
- Photos of the product (optional but recommended)

**Step 2: Obtain Return Instructions**

Our team will respond within 24 hours with:
- Return shipping address
- Return shipping instructions
- Return authorization number (if applicable)
- Courier partner information

**Step 3: Prepare Product for Return**

Ensure the product meets all eligibility criteria:
- Keep all original tags and labels intact
- Include original packaging and accessories
- Ensure product is clean and in original condition
- Pack securely to prevent damage during transit

**Step 4: Ship the Product**

- Use the provided return shipping address
- Obtain a tracking number for your return shipment
- Keep proof of shipment and tracking details
- ZEVRAE recommends using a trackable courier service

**Step 5: Quality Inspection**

Upon receipt of the returned product:
- ZEVRAE will inspect the product within 1-2 business days
- Verification of condition, tags, packaging, and eligibility
- Inspection report will be documented

**Step 6: Refund Processing**

If the product passes quality inspection:
- Refund approval notification will be sent via email
- Refund will be initiated within 3 business days of approval
- Amount will be credited to customer's account within 7 business days (subject to bank processing)

### Return Shipping Responsibility

ZEVRAE follows a **mixed approach** for return shipping costs:

#### ZEVRAE Pays Return Shipping (Free Returns)

Return shipping is covered by ZEVRAE if the return is due to:

- ✓ **Manufacturing Defect:** Product has a defect in materials or construction
- ✓ **Damage During Transit:** Product was damaged while in ZEVRAE's delivery process
- ✓ **Incorrect Item Delivered:** Wrong product was shipped
- ✓ **Discrepancy:** Product received doesn't match the order

**In these cases:**
- ZEVRAE will bear the return shipping cost
- Prepaid return labels or courier arrangements will be provided (where available)
- Customer will receive clear return instructions

#### Customer Pays Return Shipping

Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility if the return is due to:

- ✓ **Change of Mind:** Customer no longer wants the product
- ✓ **Size/Fit Issues:** Customer ordered incorrect size
- ✓ **Color/Style Preference:** Customer prefers a different color or style
- ✓ **Personal Reasons:** Any reason not attributable to ZEVRAE's error or product defect

**In these cases:**
- Customer arranges and pays for return shipping
- ZEVRAE will provide the return address
- Customer should obtain a tracking number for proof of shipment

## REFUND POLICY

### Refund Methods

Approved refunds will be processed using one of the following methods:

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **UPI Transfer** | Preferred method for faster processing |
| **Bank Transfer** | Processed if customer provides verified bank account details |

**ZEVRAE reserves the right to determine the most appropriate refund method** based on:

- Banking limitations and regulations
- Verification requirements
- Customer request and eligibility

### Refund Processing Timeline

Once a returned product is received and **successfully passes ZEVRAE's quality inspection:**

#### Refund Initiation

- **Processing Time:** Refund will be initiated within **3 business days** of approval
- **Notification:** Customer will receive an email notification once refund is processed
- **Reference Number:** A refund reference number will be provided for tracking

#### Refund Credit to Account

- **Credit Timeline:** Amount is generally credited within **7 business days** after refund is processed
- **Variation:** Actual credit timelines may vary depending on:
  - Customer's bank or payment service provider
  - Banking processing times (typically 3-5 business days for cards; 1-2 days for digital wallets)
  - Holidays and weekends

**Note:** ZEVRAE initiates the refund promptly, but the actual credit depends on the financial institution's processing timelines.

### Partial Refunds

ZEVRAE may issue a **partial refund** in certain situations:

#### Shipping Charges (Non-Refundable)

**Original shipping or delivery charges are NOT refunded** unless:

- The return is due to a **manufacturing defect**
- The return is due to an **incorrect product delivered**
- The return is due to a **damaged product received**
- Any error is **attributable to ZEVRAE**

In these cases, the full refund amount (including shipping) will be processed.

#### Restocking Fee

- **ZEVRAE does NOT charge any restocking fees** on eligible returns
- 100% of the product price is refunded (minus applicable shipping charges, if applicable)

#### Other Deductions

- **GST/Tax:** Refund amount includes applicable GST (as per invoiced amount)
- **Discount/Promo:** If a discount or promotional code was applied, the refund reflects the discounted amount
- **Gift Wrapping:** If gift wrapping charges were added, they may not be refunded (as a non-returnable service)

### Refund Status & Tracking

**To check your refund status:**

1. Check your email for refund notifications
2. Contact ZEVRAE support with your Order Number and Refund Reference Number
3. Response time: 12-24 hours

**Refund Status Information:**

## QUALITY INSPECTION STANDARDS

### Inspection Process

All returned products undergo **rigorous quality inspection** to ensure compliance with ZEVRAE's Return & Exchange Policy.

### Inspection Criteria

**Returned products must meet ALL of the following standards:**

#### 1. Tags & Labels

- ✓ All original tags must be **intact and uncut**
- ✓ No stickers removed or defaced
- ✓ Care labels must be unfolded and readable
- ✗ If cut, removed, or damaged → **Product fails inspection**

#### 2. Condition of Garment

- ✓ No visible signs of **use, wear, or handling**
- ✓ Product must appear **unused and unworn**
- ✓ No creases, fading, or discoloration from wear
- ✗ If worn or used → **Product fails inspection**

#### 3. Cleanliness

- ✓ Must be **free from stains** of any kind
- ✓ No marks, dirt, discoloration, or residue
- ✓ No odors (body odor, perfume, cigarette smoke, etc.)
- ✗ If stained or odorous → **Product fails inspection**

#### 4. Care & Alterations

- ✓ Must **not be washed or dry-cleaned**
- ✓ No alterations, hemming, or modifications
- ✓ No seams ripped or adjusted
- ✗ If washed or altered → **Product fails inspection**

#### 5. Physical Integrity

- ✓ No **tears, rips, holes, or cuts**
- ✓ No scratches, pilling, or snags
- ✓ Seams intact and undamaged
- ✓ Zippers/buttons functional and undamaged
- ✓ No color fading or damage from exposure
- ✗ If damaged → **Product fails inspection**

#### 6. Accessories & Packaging

- ✓ All **original accessories included** (tags, stickers, inserts)
- ✓ **Original packaging intact** (box, tissue, bags)
- ✓ **All complimentary items included** (sample, gift, etc.)
- ✗ If items missing or packaging damaged → **Product fails inspection**

#### 7. Item Verification

- ✓ Returned item **matches the original order** (SKU, size, color)
- ✓ Item is an **authentic ZEVRAE product**
- ✗ If item doesn't match or is counterfeit → **Product fails inspection**

### Inspection Decision

**Products that PASS inspection:**
- ✓ Eligible for full refund or exchange
- ✓ Refund initiated within 3 business days
- ✓ Refund credited within 7 business days

**Products that FAIL inspection:**
- ✗ Not eligible for refund or exchange
- ✗ Product will be returned to customer (at customer's expense)
- ✗ No refund or credit issued

### Appeal Process for Failed Inspection

If you believe your return was **incorrectly rejected:**

**Step 1: Submit Appeal**

Email zevareofficial@gmail.com within **7 days** of rejection notice with:
- Order Number
- Rejection reason
- Your explanation
- Additional photographic evidence

**Step 2: Senior Review**

- Appeal will be reviewed by senior support team
- Investigation typically takes 5-7 business days
- You'll receive a detailed response

**Step 3: Final Decision**

- If appeal is approved: Refund will be processed
- If appeal is denied: Decision is final, product can be claimed

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Return Policy Questions

**Q1: What is the difference between the return window for clothing vs. accessories?**

A: Clothing has a **2-day return window** while accessories have a **1-day return window**. This is to maintain hygiene standards, as accessories have stricter requirements. The shorter window for accessories ensures product quality and sanitation.

**Q2: Can I return an item after the return window has closed?**

A: No, returns can only be accepted within the specified window (2 days for clothing, 1 day for accessories). After the window closes, the product is ineligible for return. We recommend initiating your return as soon as possible.

**Q3: What if I initiated the return on day 2 but can't ship until day 5?**

A: The return window is based on **when you initiate the request**, not when you physically ship the product. However, we recommend shipping within 2-3 days of initiating the return to ensure the item reaches us in good condition.

**Q4: Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?**

A: Yes! Exchanges are available within the same return window (2 days for clothing, 1 day for accessories) and follow the same eligibility criteria. Exchanges are often faster than returns + refunds.

### Refund Questions

**Q5: How long does it take to receive my refund after I ship the item back?**

A: 
1. Shipping time: 3-5 business days (your courier)
2. Quality inspection: 1-2 business days
3. Refund processing: 3 business days
4. Bank/UPI processing: 3-7 business days

**Total: 10-20 business days** from when you ship until the refund appears in your account.

**Q6: Can I get a refund as store credit instead of money back?**

A: Currently, ZEVRAE offers refunds only as money back (UPI or bank transfer). Store credit is not available at this time, except during special promotional campaigns.

**Q7: If I used a discount code, what refund do I get?**

- Original Price: ₹1,000
- Discount Code: 20% off

### Return Eligibility Questions

**Q8: Can I return a product if the tags are partially removed?**

A: No. If tags are cut, removed, or partially damaged, the product fails quality inspection and is ineligible for return. Tags must be completely intact and uncut.

**Q9: What if the product has a manufacturing defect? Can I still return it?**

A: Yes! Products with manufacturing defects are always returnable, regardless of condition. Additionally, **ZEVRAE will cover return shipping** for defective items. Contact support immediately with photos of the defect.

**Q10: Can I return items from a promotional offer or sale?**

A: Items from regular sales are returnable within the standard window. However, items marked as "Final Sale" or "No Return" are ineligible. Check the product page before purchase.

**Q11: Are customized or personalized items returnable?**

A: No. Customized, personalized, or made-to-order items are **non-returnable** except where required by law. This is because they cannot be resold to other customers.
